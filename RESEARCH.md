# Research Topics & Ideas

## Tools & Frameworks

- [karpathy/autoresearch](https://github.com/karpathy/autoresearch) — Automated research tool by Andrej Karpathy. Could be useful for running onboarding experiments — e.g., researching optimal onboarding flows, drop-off patterns, and conversion strategies using automated literature review.
- [promptfoo/promptfoo](https://github.com/promptfoo/promptfoo) — LLM evaluation and testing framework. Useful for systematically testing and comparing prompt strategies.
- [666ghj/MiroFish](https://github.com/666ghj/MiroFish) — AI-powered multi-agent simulation engine that constructs high-fidelity digital worlds from real-world seed data to predict future outcomes through autonomous agent interactions and social dynamics.
